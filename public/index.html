Chart Game

You will be shown a series of ten historical price charts for different stocks from different past time periods. For each chart, you will be asked to forecast whether the stock will go up, down, or sideways in the future and how sure you are about your guess. After completing all the charts you can see how all of your predictions actually panned out. The price level and dates have been altered to make the charts anonymous, but each pattern happened in real life.
1. On a scale of 1 (low) - 5 (high), how would you rate yourself on each of the following:
a. Financial literacy
b. Financial knowledge
c. Financial experience

2. Are you a financial professional?
a. How many years of experience do you have in the field?
b. Which of the following most closely describes your current role?

3. Do you or have you ever had any personal investments?
a. Do you have a 401K or other retirement account?
b. Do you prefer investing in:
c. How would you describe your current asset allocation? (a rough guess is fine, just leave it blank if you have no idea)
d. How do you think your personal portfolio has performed over the past 2 years? (again a rough guess is fine)
